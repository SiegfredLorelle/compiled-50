More Details
  • The readability test used was Coleman-Liau Index. It considers the number of letters, words, and sentences of the paragraph.
  • It is highly encouraged to use proper spelling, grammar, and punctuation as it can affect the accuracy of the Readability.
  • Readability is prone to miscount the number of sentences when periods (.) were not used to terminate a sentence.
  • Readability will accepts everything including other languages (but better to use English) regardless of spelling, grammar and punctuation.
